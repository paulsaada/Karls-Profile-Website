About

Age: 21

Height: 6'4

Residence: New York, NY

Karl Charles is a fashion model and marketing student. He's the son of first generation Haitian-born parents, a native of New Jersey, and currently resides in NYC.

He began inquiring about fashion modeling after taking several courses/seminars in Art History on and in Visual Arts and took an interest in visuals with various body postures and facial expressions to reflect authentic distinction.

When he’s not modelling, he’s an avid book-reader interested in a wide variety of different genres including philosophy and self-development, he’s a hobbyist explorer, and he utilizes meditation and yoga to have meaningful self-reflection within himself.

“I always aim to acknowledge the uniqueness of my individuality to be the best human I can possibly be, without sacrificing my truths.”

He enjoys collaborative projects and experiences. If you'd like to work with him, you may reach him here.
